# Skill: Growth Strategy

## Trigger
Any task involving user acquisition, retention, growth metrics, or go-to-market planning

## Steps
1. Define growth stage:
   - **Pre-launch**: build waitlist, beta testers
   - **Launch**: first 100 users, feedback loop
   - **Growth**: scale to 1000+ users, paid channels
   - **Retention**: reduce churn, increase engagement
2. For each app, define:
   - North Star Metric (the ONE number that matters)
   - Acquisition channels (where users come from)
   - Activation event (what makes a user "active")
   - Retention trigger (what brings them back)
3. Tamil Nadu launch strategy:
   - Start with 50-100 beta users (friends, family, college students)
   - Collect feedback for 2 weeks
   - Fix top 5 issues
   - Expand to 500 users via WhatsApp + word of mouth
   - Then Play Store + paid ads
4. Track weekly: new users, active users, retention rate, revenue

## Example
EatWell growth:
- North Star: daily active meal loggers
- Acquisition: WhatsApp + Play Store + Instagram
- Activation: user logs first meal
- Retention: daily meal reminders via push notification

## Notes
- Don't scale before product-market fit (users love the app)
- Word of mouth is the best channel for India — make the app shareable
- College students are the best early adopters — target engineering colleges in TN
- Track everything from Day 1 — you can't improve what you don't measure
